Rapid wireframing

A native iOS app wireframing kit. It's a quick to use, component based structure to ease and speed up that initial design process.

I created a native iOS app wireframing kit. It's an easy to use, component based structure to ease and speed up that initial design process. This was inspired by the time when I was training up a junior designer to get into app design. I found that one of the most important lessons was to teach how both the iOS and Android apps differ and how the design structure works in both. Learning with native components seemed to make a lot of sense, as the saying goes - once you learn the basics and the rules you can then change and break them in a way that makes sense. It considers a variety of screen sizes and includes guidelines for each.

This kit is for anyone working with app design, however it is also a learning tool. It was a featured product on UI8 when released and it is still available to download.
